your learning path

From fundamentals to a shipped platform

Eight modules grouped into four phases — take the full curriculum or follow a focused track.

1
Foundations & Tooling
Agentic AI fundamentals, LLM APIs and tool calling against your infra — ReAct, RAG, kubectl, Terraform. (Modules 1–2)
2
Pipelines & Observability
AI in CI/CD, RAG over logs, and incident-response agents that correlate alerts and draft RCAs. (Modules 3–4)
3
IaC & Security
AI-assisted Terraform, drift remediation, guardrails and a hands-on red-team exercise. (Modules 5–6)
4
Multi-Agent & Capstone
Orchestration and MCP, then design, harden and demo a working autonomous ops platform. (Modules 7–8)
