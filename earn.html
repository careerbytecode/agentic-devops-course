The 365-Day Visibility Challenge

Visibility is a habit, not an event. Do these every day and every week for a year, and you become impossible to ignore in your niche.

Every Day

Daily Non-Negotiables

  • 1 LinkedIn post on an agent or ops idea
  • 1 short build clip or reel
  • 1 tip on automating a real ops task
  • Answer 10 comments in your niche
  • Connect with 20 engineers / hiring managers
  • Comment on 10 AI/DevOps posts
Every Week

Weekly Power Moves

  • 1 agent build or case study
  • 1 live session or workshop
  • 1 newsletter issue
  • 1 podcast or expert interview
  • 1 technical blog post
  • 1 free resource (template / checklist)

Consistency compounds. Each post, clip and session is a brick - in a year you'll have built a brand no one can take away.
